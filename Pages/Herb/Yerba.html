Yerba Buena (Mentha cordifolia)
Uses & Preparation:
• Head pain, stomachache: Pound a small piece and apply to the affected area.
• Rheumatism, arthritis, and headache: Crush fresh leaves and squeeze sap. Massage with eucalyptus.
• Cough and cold: Soak 10 fresh leaves in hot water, drink as tea.
• Swollen gums & toothache: Squeeze sap and soak cotton, insert into cavity.
